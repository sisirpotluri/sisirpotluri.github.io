Personal Project

Job Search

Used machine learning with C++ data structures such as maps and sets to train an algorithm with Google job listings. The program predicts jobs to apply for based on input such as category of work, location, and skills. After much revision, the classifier algorithm was engineered to complete training of 1700+ jobs in 1.72 seconds.
